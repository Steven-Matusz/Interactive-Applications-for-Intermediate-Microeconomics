💡 Key Concept

A two-part tariff has two pieces: (1) a fixed fee (cover charge / membership) and (2) a per-unit price. Setting the per-unit price equal to MC maximizes total output and eliminates deadweight loss. The fixed fee then captures some or all consumer surplus as profit.
