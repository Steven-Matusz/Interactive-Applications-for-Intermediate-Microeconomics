💡 Key Concept

Accounting profit = Revenue − Explicit costs (what the accountant sees).

Economic profit = Accounting profit − Opportunity cost of capital.

A firm with positive accounting profit but negative economic profit is covering its bills but failing to reward investors for the risk they bear.

📊 Accounting Profit (NOPAT)
—
NOPAT = Revenue − Operating Expenses − Taxes
💰 Economic Profit (EVA)
—
EVA = NOPAT − (Invested Capital × Required Return)
