🔑 Key Concepts

  • Point A — Original optimum: tangency of original budget line and indifference curve.
  • Price change — The budget line pivots; the consumer moves to a new optimum (Point C).
  • Substitution Effect (A→B) — Holding utility constant, the consumer shifts toward the relatively cheaper good.
  • Income Effect (B→C) — The real income change from the price change shifts the consumer from B to the final choice C.
