Key Concepts

Public Goods

Non-rivalrous: everyone consumes the same unit
Non-excludable: can't prevent free-riding
Summation: Vertical — add WTP at each quantity

Private Goods

Rivalrous: each unit goes to one person
Excludable: non-payers can be blocked
Summation: Horizontal — add quantities at each price

Samuelson Rule: Provide the public good up to the quantity where the sum of individual WTP equals marginal cost (ΣMB = MC).
