💡 The Tragedy of the Commons

When a resource is open-access, each fisherman's private decision ignores the depletion costs imposed on everyone else. The competitive equilibrium (e꜀) occurs where P = MCp, overproducing relative to the social optimum (eₛ) where P = MCs. The gap between the two curves equals the marginal external cost.
