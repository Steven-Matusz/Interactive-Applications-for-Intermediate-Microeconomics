The Scenario

Joe and Mary each own a bakery in a small town. They simultaneously choose how much bread to bake. Each takes the other's quantity as given and picks the quantity that maximises their own profit — this is the Cournot model.

Market Parameters

Demand: P = 120 − Q  (Q = qJoe + qMary)
Cournot: Each firm simultaneously picks quantity, treating the rival's output as fixed.
